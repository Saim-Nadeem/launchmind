SummarizeIt

Grasp Key Information, Save Precious Time.

SummarizeIt is a browser extension that provides concise, on-demand summaries of long articles, helping busy professionals, students, and casual readers quickly get the gist without the full read.

Key Features Designed for You

⚡

On-Demand Summary Generation

Get a concise summary of any article with a single click on the browser extension icon. Instant insights, no hassle.

⚙️

Customizable Summary Length

Tailor your summaries! Choose your desired length, from a few sentences to bullet points or a full paragraph, to suit your needs.

💡

Key Takeaways & Bullet Points

Quickly digest the main ideas and arguments. Summaries are presented in an easy-to-read bullet-point format, highlighting core concepts.

↔️

Contextual Pop-up/Sidebar Display

View summaries without leaving the page. Our non-intrusive pop-up or collapsible sidebar keeps your reading flow uninterrupted.

🌐

Multi-language Support

Expand your reach. Summarize articles written in various languages, making the tool useful for a global audience.

⏱️

Save Time, Boost Productivity

Stop wasting time on irrelevant content. Quickly identify valuable articles and focus your energy where it matters most.
