QuickRead AI: Summarize Articles Instantly.

A browser extension that summarises long articles before you read them, helping busy professionals, students, and casual readers quickly grasp key information and decide if a full read is necessary.

Add to Chrome - It's Free!

Powerful Features to Boost Your Reading Efficiency

On-Demand Summary Generation

Get a concise summary of the current web article with a single click of the extension icon.

Customizable Summary Length

Choose your desired summary length (e.g., 'short', 'medium', 'long', or a specific number of sentences/paragraphs).

Key Takeaways/Bullet Points

Quickly grasp the most important points of the article in an easy-to-digest bulleted list format.

Summary Overlay/Sidebar

Displays the summary in a non-intrusive overlay or a collapsible sidebar next to the original article content.

Multi-Language Support

Ability to summarize articles written in various languages and potentially translate the summary into the user's preferred language.
